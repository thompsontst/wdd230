Ibom Industrial City

The Ibom industrial city is a conceptualization of government of Akwa Ibom State aim at commerce and industrial activities in the state

The city which also operates as a park houses a range of business and bussiness apportunity. Its at the commercial city that all enterprenurial opportunity exists. Talents are birthed at the City park as potential buyer gets to meet

The Ibom Industrial City (IIC) is meant to create new jobs and work as a functioning and integrated new city, with housing and all required infrastructure services, integrated with the Ibom Deep Sea Port (IDSP) facility, being developed in parallel to the Industrial City.

This is encapsulated in the project vision: â€œIbom Industrial City, harnessing its strategic waterfront location, is envisaged to become an exemplar industrial city with a world-class deep-sea port, which will act as a gateway to West and Central Africa, attract global investment, foster innovation and act as a national and regional industrial and economic hub

The vision and economic development strategy for the IIC aligns with the Nigeria Vision 2020 Plan, Niger Delta Regional Development Master Plan and Nigerian Industrial Revolution Plan (NIRP).

The project site covers 14,517 hectares to the south-east of Akwa Ibom State.

The City also has recently added to her list the city business school which is in collaboration with the Lagos Bussiness school
